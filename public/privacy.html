🍛 Privacy Policy

Pantry Pandit · Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Pantry Pandit, we collect:

  • Account information — name and email address when you register via Google or Email
  • Pantry data — ingredients and quantities you enter
  • Meal plans — plans generated and saved by you
  • Shopping lists — items auto-generated from your plans

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalised AI meal plans using your pantry items
  • To save and display your meal history and shopping lists
  • To authenticate your account securely
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted services:

  • Firebase (Google) — for authentication and data storage
  • Google Gemini AI — to generate meal plan recipes (only pantry data is sent, no personal identifiers)
  • Render.com — for app hosting

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore
  • Only you can access your own data — protected by Firebase security rules
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS

5. Your Rights

  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • You can request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • You can update or correct your information within the app

6. Children's Privacy

Pantry Pandit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
